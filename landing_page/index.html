What is Meez?

Meez helps you cut through the clutter of online recipes and focus on the cooking. No more scrolling through lengthy introductions, personal anecdotes, or endless ads.

Just paste a recipe link, recipe text, or a photo of a recipe, and we'll give you the ingredients and instructions, quickly and cleanly.

We also help you substitute ingredients you've got at home and scale up recipe amounts to your needs. We make the life of a home cook easier, so you can just focus on the cooking.

How it Works

  1. Find a recipe online that you want to use.
  2. Copy the URL of the recipe page, or the text of the recipe, or a photo of the recipe.
  3. Paste it into the input bar on our Home page or directly into the Meez mobile app.
  4. Get a clutter-free recipe and start cooking. You can also sub out ingredients, change servings, and organize your recipes.
