What is Sift and Serve?

Sift and Serve is an app designed to cut through the clutter of online recipes. No more scrolling through lengthy introductions, personal anecdotes, or endless ads. Just paste a recipe URL, and we'll extract the core ingredients and instructions for you, quickly and cleanly.

How it Works

  1. Find a recipe online that you want to use.
  2. Copy the URL of the recipe page.
  3. Paste the URL into the input bar on our Home page or directly into the Sift and Serve app.
  4. Let Sift and Serve extract the essential recipe details.
  5. Enjoy your focused, ad-free recipe!
