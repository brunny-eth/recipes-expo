What is Sift and Serve?

Sift and Serve helps you cut through the clutter of online recipes and focus on the cooking.

No more scrolling through lengthy introductions, personal anecdotes, or endless ads. Just paste a recipe URL, and we'll give you the ingredients and instructions, quickly and cleanly.

We also give you useful tools, help you substitute ingredients, and scale up recipe amounts to your needs.

How it Works

  1. Find a recipe online that you want to use.
  2. Copy the URL of the recipe page.
  3. Paste the URL into the input bar on our Home page or directly into the Sift and Serve mobile app.
  4. Get a clutter-free recipe and start cooking.
